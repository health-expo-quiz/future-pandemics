Contagion: Pandemic 2050

The Story So Far...

In the year 2050, a mysterious and highly contagious pathogen emerges from the thawing permafrost in Siberia. This virus, identified as a mutated strain of the Black Death, rapidly spreads across the globe, sparking panic and chaos.

You, Dr. Aria Chen, a world-renowned epidemiologist, have been appointed as the leader of the global response to this unprecedented crisis. Your mission: contain the outbreak, develop a vaccine, and save humanity.

The challenges are immense. Climate refugees fleeing rising sea levels complicate containment efforts. Cyber attacks threaten critical vaccine research, and a growing anti-science movement undermines public trust, culminating in civil war.

The world is watching. Can you save it?
